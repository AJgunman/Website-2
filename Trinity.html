Trinity University is an excellent school in San Antonio Texas. It has a wonderful campus with lots of opportunities for students. Trinity promotes both effort and quality on campus with each class. This is part of the reason I chose Trinity. The classes here are rigorous and rewarding, each class providing its own unique challenges and lessons.

The features that I appreciate most at Trinity are the difficulty and high expectations of the courses and the challenge that they bring, and the size of the campus. I like the difficulty of the courses here as they force me to push my limits and challenge me to do better and work harder. I like the small campus size as it allows me to meet up and get to know many of my peers in a way that a larger university simply wouldn't.

I chose to attend Trinity for the reasons stated above.
